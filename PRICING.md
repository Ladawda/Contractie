## Value Proposition

### For Clients
- **Launch**: FREE job posting
- **Regular**: $10 flat fee (vs $50-100/lead elsewhere)
- Direct access to verified contractors
- No middleman markup
- You negotiate directly

### For Contractors
- **Founding**: $25/month unlimited (50% off forever)
- **Regular**: $49/month unlimited
- No per-lead fees
- No commission on jobs won
- Keep 100% of earnings
- Direct relationship with clients
